# Microcopy Guide
## Voice, Tone & Messaging

> *"Every sentence a brushstroke. No filler, no hype. Everything must feel inevitable."*

---

## VOICE CHARACTERISTICS

### Core Principles
- **Clear, precise, minimal** — Every word earns its place
- **Compassionate but not preachy** — Facts with feeling
- **Hopeful without naivety** — Realism with optimism  
- **Inviting, not demanding** — "Join us" not "You must"
- **Serene confidence** — Truth without aggression

---

## TONE MATRIX

### Hero Messages

#### ✅ APPROVED
- "99% Vegan Germany. Transparency is Love."
- "Every Euro Has a Story."
- "We Can Live Beautifully Without Harm."
- "The Future is Plant-Based. The Future is Now."
- "Follow the Flow. See the Truth."

#### ❌ AVOID
- "Stop Animal Cruelty Now!" (too aggressive)
- "The Government is Lying!" (too angry)
- "You Must Act Now!" (too demanding)
- "Save the Animals!" (too emotional manipulation)

### 5. ERROR & LOADING STATES

#### Loading
- "Loading..." (simple)
- "Gathering insights..." (contextual)
- "Preparing the story..." (poetic but brief)

#### Errors
*Helpful, not apologetic, actionable*

- "Something went wrong. Please try again."
- "Data unavailable. Check back soon."
- "Connection lost. Retrying..."

#### 404 / Not Found
- "This page doesn't exist. Return to the flow."
- "Page not found. Let's start over."

- Truth

- Choice
- Vision
- Impact
- Flow
- Connection
- Harmony
- Understanding
- Change
- Movement
- Journey

### ❌ Avoid
- Cruelty (too harsh)
- Suffering (use sparingly, with context)
- Fight / Battle (too aggressive)
- Victory / Win (too competitive)
- Demand (too forceful)
- Must / Should (too prescriptive)
- Urgent (too pushy)
- Shame / Guilt (never)

## SENTENCE STRUCTURE

### Guidelines
1. **Short sentences** — Maximum 20 words
2. **Active voice** — "We create change" not "Change is created"
3. **Positive framing** — "We can" not "We must stop"
4. **Concrete over abstract** — "Plant-based foods" not "Ethical consumption"

### Example Transformation

❌ **Before**:
> "We must urgently stop the cruel and inhumane treatment of animals in factory farms throughout Germany, and if you don't act now, millions of innocent creatures will continue to suffer horrible deaths."

✅ **After**:
> "Germany can lead the way. Zero suffering. 100% plant-based. Together, we create a world where compassion guides our choices."

## CONSISTENCY CHECKLIST

Before publishing any copy, verify:

- [ ] Does this sound like our voice? (Clear, compassionate, hopeful)
- [ ] Is every word necessary?
- [ ] Does it inspire action through beauty, not guilt?
- [ ] Is it accessible? (Clear language, no jargon)
- [ ] Does it align with our values? (Love, truth, liberation)
- [ ] Would this make someone feel "This is the future of kindness"?
- [ ] Is the tone serene and confident, not aggressive?

---

**Remember**: Every sentence is a brushstroke. Design with words as you would with pixels. Minimal. Precise. Inevitable.
