## 📖 The Stories We Tell

### Story 1: The Generational Transfer (Social Security)
**The Insight**: 44.5% of YOUR tax money goes to social welfare - €1,841 per person per year.

**Visuals**:
- Beautiful gradient card with per-day breakdown
- Donut chart showing pension/unemployment/family split
- Real numbers: 13.7M pensioners, 17.4M total served
- Personal hook: €5.04 per day from YOUR pocket

**Discussion Starter**: "Is 44% too much, or just right for a welfare state?"

---

### Story 2: The Ukraine Response (Defense Surge)
**The Insight**: Defense spending exploded +37.5% since 2018 - the most dramatic shift.

**Visuals**:
- Timeline chart showing 2018 → 2024 growth
- Before/after comparison (€37.9B → €52.1B)
- Personal impact: €0.06 per person per day for military
- Context: NATO commitments, Ukraine aid

**Discussion Starter**: "Was this necessary, or an overreaction?"

### Story 3: Betting on the Future (Education)

- Context: Germany's competitive edge
