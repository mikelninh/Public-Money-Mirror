## 🎯 Design Principles Applied

### Visual Hierarchy
- **Hero Stats:** Large, bold numbers that grab attention
- **Gradient Cards:** Distinct colors help differentiate information
- **Consistent Spacing:** Professional padding and margins throughout

### Color Psychology
- **Blue Gradients:** Trust, stability (hero, personal stats)
- **Purple/Pink:** Creativity, innovation (top category)
- **Amber/Gold:** Warmth, trends, time (trends tab)
- **Green:** Growth, comparison (compare tab)

### Typography
- **Hero Title:** 64px, weight 900, letter-spacing -3px
- **Statistics:** 42-48px, weight 900
- **Body Text:** 16-18px, line-height 1.8
- **Labels:** 13-14px uppercase, letter-spacing 0.5-1.5px

### Interactive Elements
- **Hover Effects:** Cards with transform and shadow changes
- **Animations:** Float, pulse, slideIn effects
- **Layering:** z-index management for depth
